Grilled Lemon Herb Chicken

Description:

Juicy grilled chicken marinated with lemon and herbs for a fresh, flavorful meal.

Prep Time: 10 mins, Cook Time: 20 mins, Total Time: 30 mins

Cuisine: American
Keyword: grilled chicken, lemon herb chicken
Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 2 large chicken breasts
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix olive oil, lemon juice, garlic powder, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  2. Marinate chicken in the mixture for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Grill chicken for about 8-10 minutes per side, until fully cooked.
  5. Let rest for 5 minutes before serving.
